Frequently asked questions

What is unclaimed property?

Dormant funds held by states or institutions — e.g., old bank accounts, insurance payouts, deposits, or overpayments.

What do you charge?

A contingent fee disclosed up front. If you don’t recover funds, you don’t pay a fee.

How long does it take?

Varies by agency and documentation; many claims resolve in weeks to a few months.

Can I file myself?

Yes. You may file directly with your state’s unclaimed property office without using a paid service.

What documents might be required?

Typically government ID, address verification, and proof of relationship to the owner or entity.

How do you handle my data?

We collect only what’s needed to evaluate and process your claim. See our Privacy Policy.
