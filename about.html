Returning unclaimed property to rightful owners.

We exist to make recovery simple, respectful, and compliant—particularly for families, small businesses, nonprofits, and heirs.

Mission

Our mission is to identify dormant assets, verify entitlement, and file accurate, timely claims so funds are released to those who earned them. We emphasize clarity, ethics, and measurable results.

Compliance

  • We operate within applicable state and federal law and consumer-protection rules.
  • We clearly disclose that claimants may file directly with their state without using a paid service.
  • We handle only the minimum personal information required to evaluate and process a claim.
