Privacy Policy

We collect only what’s necessary to evaluate and process your claim. We do not sell your information.

Information we collect

Contact details and claim-related information you submit through our intake form. We do not request sensitive documents through the website.

How we use it

To evaluate eligibility, contact you, and prepare a claim if you decide to proceed.

Retention

We retain intake submissions up to 12 months unless a service agreement is signed, in which case applicable records may be retained longer for legal and accounting purposes.

Security

We use HTTPS, least-privilege access, and minimal data collection. No method is 100% secure online, but we apply reasonable measures.

Your choices

Contact us to correct or delete your information where applicable.
